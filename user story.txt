As a user,
I want to create and manage personalized playlists,
So that I can easily listen to my favorite songs without manually selecting them each time.

As a user,
I want to download my favorite songs for offline playback,
So that I can listen to music even without an internet connection.

As a user,
I want to adjust the volume and customize the sound with equalizer settings,
So that I can personalize my listening experience based on my preferences.

As a user,
I want to search for songs, albums, or artists,
So that I can quickly find new music or specific tracks in my library.
